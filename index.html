About Me

I'm Shunina, a freelance artist from Argentina.

I have been drawing since I was a little kid, I've been into videogames for as long as I can remember and a big fan of anime, and have been watching it since I was a kid.

There's something about creating something from nothing that I find incredibly satisfying. Whether it's a painting, a drawing or something else entirely, I love the challenge of taking an idea and making it a reality. I also enjoy the process of experimentation- trying out new techniques, seeing what works and what doesn't. I'm always looking to push my boundaries and see how far I can take my art. I'm an artist who likes to bring ideas to people on drawings.

I'm also fluent in Spanish and English, which allows me to work with clients from all over the world...

I hope we can work togheter!
